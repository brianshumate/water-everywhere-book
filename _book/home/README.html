Singing Trees

So far smears of gold and
smoky oranges then shocking
red,

I take a selphie or two for you
on the deck in their midst.
leaves exploding with color
like the thoughts inside my head.

When the winds meet our
ridge they're singing
trees and swaying at their
tree knees
just like the way I do to you
and the way that you do to me.

You can glance out there and
watch them belt out a tune
about the stars and the moon

shaking off leaves for this
time that you will
gradually appreciate.

Like all of the fine
music you have shared
with me,

I am here
a native
ready to
ease you
into cold

until singing trees
return again green.

My Spot

The Earth will occasionally
graciously agree

to spend a little time with me

sitting next to an ancient
oak tree

at my spot.

With sweet breeze blowing through
the leaves of that tree
my focus will often wander

to thee.

Breaths refocus and gently remind
the primary purpose of spending
this time at my spot is to lose

every thought.

that I am not
alone feels more
genuine than any

gentlest

Bistro

On the interrelation of
years, months & d a y s . . .

For years—
    intending to have a
        taste and try this
        place otherwise, we
        arrive and flow right
        through.

In months—
    we have gone from
        divergent paths through
        space and time only to
        find our way back
        together.

For days—
    we meet these ways and
        amaze ourselves with
        a list that will never
        be completed.

Dining with you on this night
felt like being warmed by a
tiny sun sitting just feet
away and beaming for
a million miles.

You made the evening special,
utmost grace and enchantment
during every single minute—

Your looks,
     those looks

loop in my mind
tracing smiles
reminding me of
a cherished gift.
